  • Lease the asset. Release the potential.

  • Working Capital Financing
  • Get your cash working for you, not your suppliers.

Buy to let mortgages

Buy to let mortgages that let you buy quickly, and more cost effectively A landlord's live can be pretty hectic at times. You've usually got enough on your plate dealing with rental agents, tenants and building maintenance teams without worrying month by month about your buy to let mortgage shooting through the roof because of interest rate hikes. So, the good news is that at the Murabaha Club we don't charge interest. Instead we agree a set fee, and we pin that fee to the floor. Throughout the life of your loan that fee will never raise. Not one penny. We've made our loans easy to manage and even easier to set up. You can apply for a buy-to-let mortgage between £70,000 - £500,000 in minutes and get the funds within three days, providing:
  1. You're a UK resident aged 18yrs or over
  2. Your personal income is at least £30,000 per annum
  3. You are NOT a first time buyer
  4. The loan is not for a new build
  5. You are not purchasing off-plan or investing in commercial property
  6. The value of the property you wish to purchase exceeds £100,000
  7. You can provide proof that you are an experienced landlord
  8. The rental coverage of the property will exceed 125% of the required loan repayment
  9. You pass all legal UK credit, anti-fraud and anti-money laundering checks
  10. The property is based in England or Wales
